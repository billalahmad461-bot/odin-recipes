Chicken Karahi

Description

Chicken Karahi is a classic Pakistani and North Indian dish, cooked in a wok-like pan with tender chicken, tomatoes, ginger,

garlic, and a blend of bold spices. Its rich, spicy, and slightly tangy flavor makes it a favorite for gatherings, best enjoyed with naan or steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Green chilies
  • Yogurt
  • Red chilie powder
  • Turmetric
  • Coriander powder
  • Cumin
  • Garam masala
  • Fresh coriander
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a wok/karahi and add chicken pieces. Fry until they change color.
  2. garlic and ginger, cook for a minute until fragrant.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft and mushy.
  4. Mix in red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, cumin, and salt. Stir well.
  5. Add yogurt and cook until the oil starts to separate.
  6. Toss in green chilies and cook the chicken until fully done.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander.
  8. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Nehari

Description

Nehari is a flavorful, slow-cooked meat stew from the Indian subcontinent, famous for its melt-in-your-mouth tender beef or mutton.

It is prepared in a thick, rich gravy, often enhanced with bone marrow, and seasoned with a blend of aromatic spices. Traditionally

served for breakfast, this celebratory dish is typically enjoyed with naan and garnished with fresh ginger, cilantro, and lemon.

Sindhi Biryani

Description

Sindhi Biryani is a flavorful rice dish from Sindh, Pakistan, made with layers of spiced meat, yogurt, and fragrant basmati rice.

Potatoes, fried onions, green chilies, and fresh herbs add depth, while lemon brings a tangy balance to its rich and spicy taste.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Onions
  • Potatoes
  • Green chilies
  • Fresh herbs (coriander and mint)
  • Lemon
  • Biryani masala

Steps

  1. Cook rice until almost done, set aside.
  2. Fry onions until golden, keep some for garnish.
  3. Cook meat with yogurt, spices, and potatoes until tender.
  4. Layer rice and meat mixture with fried onions, herbs, and green chilies.
  5. Steam (dum) until flavors blend.
  6. Garnish with lemon and serve hot.
